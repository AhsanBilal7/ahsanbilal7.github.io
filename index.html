Ahsan Bilal

I am a second-year PhD student in Computer Science at University of Oklahoma, advised by Dr. Dean Hougen. My research focuses on machine learning, deep learning, large language models, and explainable AI.
Previously, I earned my Bachelor of Engineering in Electrical Engineering from National University of Sciences & Technology (NUST) in Islamabad, where I was fortunate to work with Dr. Ahmad Salman.
I have industry experience as a Machine Learning Engineer at Cowlar Design Studio (YC ’21), and as a UX/UI Designer at Meraki-IT. I also delivered a talk on “AI in Healthcare” at Norman Regional Hospital under Dr. Lubna Mirza.
